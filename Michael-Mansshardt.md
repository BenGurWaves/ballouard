## Brand Overview 
Michael Manßhardt has been active in the watchmaking craft with great passion and precision for over 18 years, specializing in the restoration of historical and antique watches. His expertise in French pendulums, officer's watches, and carillon watches is particularly valued. Since 2022, he has represented the German Chamber of Crafts as an 'Ambassador of Craftsmanship,' engaging in promoting young talent and strengthening awareness for traditional watchmaking. Additionally, he manufactures customized prototypes for watches and watch-like instruments. Since 2025, he has supported the field service team with his technical expertise and passion for watch technology.
