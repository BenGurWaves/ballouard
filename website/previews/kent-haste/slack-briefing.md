### Anti-Template Enforcement
- ❌ NO centered text blocks (left-align only)
- ❌ NO symmetric columns (asymmetric 45/55, 55/45 splits)
- ❌ NO card grids in rows
- ❌ NO bullet lists (elegant typography only)
- ✅ Full-bleed imagery with gradient overlays
- ✅ Massive whitespace on single side
- ✅ Typography at architectural scale
- ✅ Continuous scroll narrative

### Brand Identity Elevated

**Color Palette:**
