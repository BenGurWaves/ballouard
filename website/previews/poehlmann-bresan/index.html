Heritage

Josef Bresan and Lukas Pöhlmann develop and manufacture their own wristwatches in traditional construction, in an atelier a short journey from Glashütte.

To take an industrial ebauche calibre and bring to market an exquisitely finished piece that honors the traditions of Saxon watchmaking.

Kaliber JU26-01 · Hand-Finished
The Movement

Based on the Soviet Pobeda calibre 2608. Completely transformed. A metamorphosis of horological alchemy.

Skeletonized train bridge revealing the double-layered gear architecture. Hand-applied anglage. 14k gold eccentric adjusting screws. Every surface finished by hand.
