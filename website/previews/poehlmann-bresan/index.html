Tradition

Unweit des Großen Gartens in Dresden, Josef Bresan and Lukas Pöhlmann develop and manufacture their own wristwatches in traditional construction. "Nach alter Väter Sitte" — according to the custom of old fathers.

Their atelier stands as a continuation of Saxony's storied watchmaking heritage, just a short journey from Glashütte, the epicenter of German horology.

"To take an industrial ebauche calibre and bring to market an exquisitely finished piece that honors the traditions of Saxon watchmaking."
