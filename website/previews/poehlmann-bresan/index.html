Heritage

Saxon Watchmaking
Tradition

Unweit des Großen Gartens in Dresden, Josef Bresan and Lukas Pöhlmann develop and manufacture wristwatches in traditional construction. Nach alter Väter Sitte.

Their atelier continues Saxony's storied watchmaking legacy, a short journey from Glashütte, the epicenter of German horology.

"To take an industrial ebauche calibre and bring to market an exquisitely finished piece that honors the traditions of Saxon watchmaking."

Kaliber JU26-01

The Movement

Based on the Soviet Pobeda calibre 2608, completely transformed. Skeletonized train bridge revealing double-layered gear architecture. Hand-applied anglage. A metamorphosis of horological alchemy.
