Heritage

Josef Bresan and Lukas Pöhlmann develop and manufacture their own wristwatches in traditional construction.

Their atelier continues Saxony's storied watchmaking legacy, a short journey from Glashütte, the epicenter of German horology.

The Movement

Based on the Soviet Pobeda calibre 2608. Completely transformed.

Skeletonized train bridge revealing double-layered gear architecture. Hand-applied anglage. 14k gold eccentric adjusting screws.

To take an industrial ebauche calibre and bring to market an exquisitely finished piece that honors the traditions of Saxon watchmaking.
