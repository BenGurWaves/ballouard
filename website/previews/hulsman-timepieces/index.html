Our Philosophy

Invention, Craftsmanship, Freedom & People

Every Hulsman timepiece is a masterpiece of precision, with nearly every component crafted by hand. From my atelier in Heerde, Gelderland, I create watches that embody years of dedication, perseverance, and passion.

Clients are deeply involved in the creative process, making each watch uniquely personal. This is not merely watchmaking—it is the preservation of a timeless art form, honoring tradition while creating something wholly unique.

The Invention

The Easy Perpetual

The world's first truly user-friendly perpetual calendar. Set the date via the crown—forward or backward—and the day, month, and leap year adjust automatically. No correctors. No tools. No risk.

  • Sapphire prisms display month & day on the caseband
  • Floating balance wheel beneath sapphire bridge
  • 90+ hours power reserve
  • Aventurine day-night indicator
  • Côtes de Genève finishing with mirror-polished anglage
