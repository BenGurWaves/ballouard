Since 1946

Three Generations of Milanese Excellence

Augusto Caraceni opened his atelier at Via Fatebenefratelli 16 in 1946, after returning from Paris where he had dressed the most prominent celebrities of the era. His son Mario carried the tradition forward, earning the prestigious Sant'Omobono prize.

Today, Massimiliano and Valentina—the fourth generation—continue the family legacy, maintaining the strict standards that have dressed Nobel laureates and fashion icons alike.
