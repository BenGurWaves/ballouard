01 / The Mind

The Physicist’s Hands

Before he was a watchmaker, Maciej was a scientist. With a PhD in physical sciences and a background in technical physics, he views time through the lens of refraction, thin layers, and quantum precision.

He walked away from the laboratory to apply this rigor to the most complex mechanical micro-systems known to man.

03 / The Legacy

The Seaman’s Chronometer

A tribute to his father, a seaman who was always on the horizon. Maciej built the marine chronometer as a love letter to heritage—reimagining the tools of navigation through the eyes of a fifth-generation craftsman.
