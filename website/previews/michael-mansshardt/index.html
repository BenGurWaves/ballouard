Michael Mansshardt

Master Watchmaker & Restorer of Historical Timepieces

The Art of Time

For over 18 years, Michael Mansshardt has dedicated his passion and precision to the intricate world of watchmaking. Specializing in the restoration of historical and antique watches, his work is a testament to timeless craftsmanship and unparalleled attention to detail.

His esteemed expertise spans French pendulums, rare officer's watches, and complex carillon mechanisms, breathing new life into horological masterpieces.

Precision & Heritage

Beyond restoration, Michael is an innovator, crafting bespoke prototypes for watches and unique horological instruments. As an 'Ambassador of Craftsmanship' for the German Chamber of Crafts, he champions the future of traditional watchmaking, fostering new talent and preserving invaluable heritage.

Each piece he touches is not merely repaired, but reborn, imbued with a meticulous blend of historical accuracy and contemporary finesse.
