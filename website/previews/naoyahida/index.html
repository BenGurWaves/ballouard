01 — Handcrafted

Human Touch

Every component shaped by master craftsmen using techniques passed through generations. From hand-polished cases to dial indices placed with tweezers — the warmth of human touch remains visible in each finished piece.

02 — Precision

Modern Technology

CNC machining and laser cutting achieve tolerances within microns — perfection impossible by hand alone. We manufacture cases, dials, and hands in-house, allowing experimentation beyond conventional limits.

03 — Vision

Unprecedented

We challenge watchmaking norms, creating timepieces that exist outside established categories. The result feels both familiar and entirely new — honoring the past while embracing the future.
