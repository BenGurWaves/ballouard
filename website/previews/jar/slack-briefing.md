## 📊 The Gap — Why This Matters

**Current State:**
- **ZERO digital footprint** — no website, no social media, no email, no online presence of any kind
- Entry to atelier: By sponsorship of existing client only
- Philosophy: Let the work speak for itself

**The Problem:**
- New-generation UHNW collectors (tech/crypto wealth, 30-50 age bracket) discover luxury brands through digital research first
- They hear "JAR" at dinner parties → search Google → find nothing confirming JAR exists
- Mystery becomes confusion becomes lost opportunity
- Current collector base aging; generational handoff at risk

**The Opportunity:**
Create the world's most exclusive digital presence — not a "website," but an online Somerset House exhibition (2002). Flashlight aesthetic, scroll reveals, total mystery maintained.

### Anti-Template Enforcement
- ❌ NO centered text blocks (left align only)
- ❌ NO symmetric columns (asymmetric 55/45, 40/60 splits)
- ❌ NO dark backgrounds (Spring ivory/cream/sage)
- ❌ NO card grids or bullet points
- ✅ Full-bleed imagery with gradient overlays
- ✅ Massive whitespace on single side
- ✅ Typography at architectural scale (12vw headlines)
- ✅ Continuous narrative flow (no section breaks)
