J.A.R. Joel Arthur Rosenthal

7 Place Vendôme

Not jewelry.
Wearable sculpture.

Since 1977, Joel Arthur Rosenthal has created fewer than 4,000 pieces — each one unique, each one commissioned for a specific client, each one the result of hundreds or thousands of hours of hand fabrication.

There are no collections. No lines. No catalog. Only individual works of art that happen to be worn on the body.

"The Matisse of our time." — Ellen Barkin
70

Pieces per year

In 48 years, approximately 4,000 works exist in the world. Fewer than some houses produce in a single month. Rosenthal's pavé technique uses gems as fine as needlepoint stitches.

1

Of one

Every piece is unique. There are no reproductions, no second editions. Each exists once, for one client. Entry to the atelier is by sponsorship of a known client only.
