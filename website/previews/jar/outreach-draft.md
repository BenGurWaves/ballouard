   - Flashlight aesthetic = scroll reveals
   - Darkness = black background, minimal text

2. **"Preserve the mystique, fix the discovery"**
   - Current problem: New UHNW can't confirm JAR exists
   - Solution: Single page that only appears for direct searches
   - No social media, no ads, no SEO gaming

3. **"Control the narrative"**
   - Currently: Auction houses tell JAR's story
   - Opportunity: Own the narrative completely
   - No third-party interpretation

4. **"By appointment only — digitally"**
   - Inquiry mechanism maintains sponsorship requirement
   - Modal states: "Existing clients may introduce new inquiries"
   - No direct contact info displayed

**Mitigation:** Offer immediate destruction of preview if unwanted. Frame as "disposable concept" — no attachment, no pressure.

**Risk:** Physical letter ignored
**Mitigation:** Unique, high-quality stationery. Wax seal. Hand-addressed. Make the letter itself feel like an artifact.
