Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Calyvent, operating under the trade name Velocity ("Company," "we," "us"), collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our website and services at velocity.delivery, velocity.calyvent.com, or any associated domains. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your data responsibly.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide directly:

  • Email address when contacting us
  • Business name, website URL, and project requirements when submitting a project inquiry
  • Communications and correspondence related to your project

Information collected automatically:

  • IP address and approximate geographic location
  • Browser type, operating system, and device information
  • Pages visited and referring URLs

Information we do not collect:

  • Credit card or banking details (processed exclusively by Stripe)
  • Social security numbers or government-issued identification

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: To design, develop, and deploy your website
  • Communication: To send project updates and respond to inquiries
  • Service improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our offerings
  • Legal compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your information for targeted advertising, profiling, or automated decision-making.

4. Payment Processing

All payment transactions are processed by Stripe, Inc. We never receive, store, or have access to your full credit card number or banking details. Stripe is PCI DSS Level 1 certified. For information on how Stripe handles your data, please review Stripe's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only with the following service providers, strictly as necessary:

  • Stripe, Inc. — Payment processing
  • Cloudflare, Inc. — Website hosting and content delivery

6. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Cloudflare's global infrastructure with encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS on all connections) and encryption at rest. While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission is 100% secure.

7. Cookies

This website does not use tracking, analytics, or advertising cookies. If authentication is required for any dashboard feature, a single HttpOnly session cookie may be set containing only a random session identifier, not personal data.

8. Data Retention

  • Project data: Retained for 1 year after project completion
  • Payment records: Retained as required by applicable tax regulations

9. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or request portability of your personal data. To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@calyvent.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be communicated via email or through a notice on our website. Your continued use of the service after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or requests, contact us at hello@calyvent.com.
