About Thompson & Sons

Founded in 1987 by Robert Thompson, our company has grown from a small two-man crew into one of Phoenix's most trusted roofing contractors. We specialize in both residential and commercial roofing projects of all sizes.

We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured (ROC #123456). All of our crew members undergo extensive safety training and background checks for your peace of mind.
